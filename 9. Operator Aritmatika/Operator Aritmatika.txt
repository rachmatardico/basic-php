OPERATOR ARITMATIKA
    - +$variable                =>>     Positif
    - -$variable                =>>     Negatif
    - $variable + $variable     =>>     Penjumlahan
    - $variable - $variable     =>>     Pengurangan
    - $variable * $variable     =>>     Perkalian
    - $variable / $variable     =>>     Pembagian
    - $variable % $variable     =>>     Sisa bagi
    - $variable ** $variable    =>>     Pangkat
